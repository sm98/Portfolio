About Me

I am a UX designer based in Bengaluru with a Master's Degree in Human Centered Design from Srishti Manipal.

I have always been passionate about interactions between humans and machines. I love working on challenging multidisciplinary projects. My background in Computer Engineering (Undergraduate) and Electronics (Junior College) enables me to work on tangible and intangible products and services.

As a part of my Engineering college's technical team, I got the opportunity to work on various projects related to Robotics, App development, Web development and Game development. While working on these projects, I realized that my interest lies in creating different experiences using technology as a tool.
